About Us

We are a non-profit Organisation which aims at helping the underprivileged section of the society. We have students as volunteers from various schools and colleges across India. We believe that the more generously you donate to help the poor, the more good Karma comes your way. We aim at making a difference in the society.

Even a small amount of donation will help in making the change.
